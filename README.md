Healthcare Stars Rating Analysis
Overview
Excel-based analysis of healthcare quality measure compliance and CMS Stars Rating performance across markets and payers.
Analysis Includes

Compliance Rate Calculation — Calculated compliance rates for BCS, CBP, COA-PNS, and DDM measures across 9 markets using patient-level data
Stars Scorecard — Built star rating models for two payers; identified performance trends and compared payer-level outcomes
Gap to Next Star Rating — Quantified the number of additional compliant patients needed to increase each measure by one star tier
Year-over-Year Delta Analysis — Compared 2022 vs 2023 market performance and surfaced key improvement trends
Strategic Recommendations — Prioritized quick wins and long-term improvement opportunities based on gap analysis

Tools Used
Excel (COUNTIFS, pivot tables, dynamic formulas), PowerPoint
Key Insights

COA-PNS improved in 8 of 9 markets YoY, making it the most consistently improving measure
CBP showed the weakest trend, declining in 5 of 9 markets
Markets 3 and 6 demonstrated the strongest broad-based improvement, suggesting potential best-practice markets
